In my second year college independent project module I produced my largest scale project to test first year students.
It quizzes users on their knowledge of their first year exam module using unique user accounts.
The question are picked in a random order so no two quizzes are the same.
Scores are recored to be displayed on a sorted scoreboard.

Volunteer Shift Management System


In the summer of 2024 I developed a system for volunteers to sign onto shifts in React Native.
Volunteers can sign onto shifts appropriate for their role and see information about their shifts.
They can then do training for their shifts which can be reading legislation or watching a training video.

Python Machine Learning Program


In the summer of 2024 I also developed a machine learning program to make predictions based on provided data.
The early version was used to predict the preferred music taste of a person based on their age and gender.
The more complicated version predicts the price of plane tickets based on trends over periods of time.
